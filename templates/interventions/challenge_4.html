Key Points

Please review the following information about the intervention.

  • This intervention is a research-based online program designed to promote physical activity in adults with obesity.
  • The intervention aims to help adults with obesity: (a) learn the benefits of physical activity, (b) find motivation for physical activity, (c) understand how to be physically active, and (d) overcome their unique barriers to physical activity.
  • To overcome the barriers to physical activity, it is strongly recommended that you increase your beliefs about your ability to be physically active, known as physical activity self-efficacy. This intervention is designed to enhance physical activity self-efficacy.
  • Based on behavioral theory and scientific evidence, the intervention provides capability-enhancing opportunities through the following engaging activities: (a) listening to lectures, (b) making action plans, (c) watching videos, (d) reflecting on success, and (e) practicing behavioral change techniques.
